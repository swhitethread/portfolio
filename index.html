Workshop Confidence

I am Samantha Whitethread, a multidisciplinary maker of things. I am a second year Design student at Goldsmiths University. I have a wide and varied skillset, and pride myself on my ability to adapt to meet a projects needs and requirements. I enjoy speculations, often through materials, and believe that a problem is best solved through making. I worked as a costume design intern on the project ‘The Human Sensor’ by artist Kasia Molga, commissioned by Invisible Dust, a non profit arts and environmental organisation. My task involved sewing together costumes and masks, preparing them for their performances.
